INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY — StartupXYZ (draft v0.3)
======================================================
Author: Head of Engineering | Status: Draft

We take security seriously. All employees must follow these rules:

1. PASSWORDS
   - Use a strong password (min 8 characters) for your laptop and all work accounts.
   - Do not share passwords with colleagues.
   - Enable two-factor authentication on your email and GitHub accounts.

2. DEVICES
   - Full disk encryption must be enabled on all laptops (FileVault on Mac,
     BitLocker on Windows).
   - Lock your screen when leaving your desk.
   - Do not install software that hasn't been approved by engineering.

3. DATA HANDLING
   - Do not store customer data on personal devices or personal cloud storage
     (Google Drive personal, Dropbox, etc.).
   - Customer data must only be processed in our AWS production environment.
   - Do not send customer data via email or Slack unless it is anonymised.

4. INCIDENTS
   - If you think you've been hacked, lost a device, or clicked a phishing link,
     tell the Head of Engineering immediately (security@startupxyz.io).
   - We'll figure it out together. Don't wait.

5. REMOTE WORK
   - Use the company VPN when connecting to production systems from public Wi-Fi.
   - Do not access production databases from personal machines.

6. CODE AND REPOSITORIES
   - Do not commit secrets (API keys, passwords, tokens) to source control.
   - Use environment variables or our secrets manager (AWS Secrets Manager) instead.
   - All code changes go through pull request review before merging to main.

7. OFFBOARDING
   - When someone leaves the company, IT revokes their access on their last day.

That's it for now. This policy will be updated as we grow.
Last updated: March 2025
